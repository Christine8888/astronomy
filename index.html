Interactive Hertzprung-Russell Diagram

GRISM Summmer 2019

Instructions:
Some stars have already been filled out, especially red and white dwarfs, which are significantly dimmer.
First, go to the website staratlas.com.
Click on a star and select show more. Enter the absolute magnitude into the absolute magnitude box.
Next, look at the spectral type. Enter the first letter into the letter box. Enter the next character, which should be a number, into the number box. Ignore any other characters.
Click on Create Star, and repeat with as many stars as you can to create a full H-R diagram.
